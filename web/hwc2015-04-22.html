Tantek Çelik:

by corporate web we are referring to social media silos like facebook, twitter g+ instagram etc

rather than create an account on some corporation's site, have your own domain, but be able to do the same things

we do the things like tweets or likes or invites and rsvps on our sites, and send copies to the corporate sites

see http://indiewebcamp.com/site-deaths for all the corporate sites that have lost people's data
