Kyle Mahan:

I wanted to add book reviews to my site and POSSE those to goodreads.com

so to do that I added goodreads support to silo.pub so I can post to goodreads with micropub

Instead of implementing a new plugin to publish to goodreads, I just add a micropub target based on silo.pub

I want to publish a new review, so I do that here and hit publish and it is POSSEd to goodreads asynchronously

the micropub edit standard isn't stable yet so silo.pub doesn't pass it through
